Gomoku

Gomoku , from Gomoku Narabe ( Kanji  :五目並べ, literally, "  aligning five pieces  ") is the Japanese name of a chinese board game , where he was appointed Wǔzi qí (五子棋, literally, "  the chessboard of 5  ") consisting of aligning 5 pawns on the intersections of the board game of go (or wéiqí, 围棋). You can play gomoku online free, on compete on multiplayers whith friends and family. It is also known in Europe under the Greek name of " pente " and specifically "Darpion" in France. Discovered in the last century by the Anglo-Saxons, the gomoku (pronounced "gomokou") has millions of followers in the Far East (China, Korea, Japan). This game of alignment, using the same grid- shaped board as the game of go, has like this Chinese origins: it is the very old game of Wǔzi qí (五子棋, "  the chessboard of the 5  "), adopted by the Koreans, who named it omok (오목). They introduced it in their turn into the Japanese empire, where this relatively simple game was considered a preparatory step to the subtleties of the game of go. A revised version, renju( connected pearls ), was born in 1931 on the initiative of the Japanese championTakagi Rakazan

Gomoku rules

The gomoku online or on the board is played on a gridded tray of 19 horizontal lines and 19 vertical lines forming, like a game of go, 361 intersections. However, the number of tokens is much lower, since players receive only 60, which they place one by one and in turn on the intersections. The gomoku player who has chosen or obtained by a draw the black pawns (and who is called Black by convention) always plays first by placing his first pawn on the central intersection of the checkerboard. White must then place his pawn on one of the 8 intersections adjacent to the black pawn. Black does the same, and so on, the goal of the game being to take the opponent's speed and succeed first in aligning 5 pieces of his color in the three possible directions: vertical, horizontal or diagonal.

How to play Gomoku

If the two gomoku players place all their pawns without any one being able to realize an alignment, the game is declared null, and they start again. A game usually takes place in two heats, so that the one who started with the blacks has the whites the second time. here is a small gomoku online tutorial (video) to understand how to play the game  

Variants of Gomoku

The renju is played on a small-sized game board, with 15 horizontal lines and 15 vertical lines forming 225 intersections. The rules, analogous to those of the gomoku , have undergone some modifications intended to diminish the advantage of the one who plays the first: these are restrictions which therefore concern only Black.
  • He is not allowed to make a double series of 3 or 4 pawns aligned or separated by a single intersection (what is called a "three-three" or a "four-four").
  • He is also prohibited alignments of more than 5 pieces, for example by joining 2 and 3 or 4 gomoku pieces already in the row to obtain a six or a seven .
